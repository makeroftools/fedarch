### Why This Matters

| Reason | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Season Identity** | Each season gets its own numeric identity — S004 = 4xy, S005 = 5xy, etc. |
| **At-a-Glance Recognition** | `L-468` immediately identifies as a S004 artifact |
| **Namespace Clarity** | Prevents ambiguity between legacy and current rules |
| **Future-Proofing** | S005 will use 5xy without conflicting with S004's 4xy |
| **Audit Trail** | Season-specific numbering enables chronological + seasonal tracing |

## 📋 The Solution — Season-Aligned Numbering

### Core Rule

> **L-468:** Season-Aligned Governance Numbering — All S004 governance artifacts (L, R, BP, D) MUST use the `4xy` sequence (e.g., L-401 to L-499, R-401 to R-499). Legacy sequences (L-2xy, L-3xy) are closed for NEW artifacts. Future seasons will use their respective season number (e.g., S005 = 5xy, S006 = 6xy).

### How It Works

| Season | Numbering Track | Range | Status |
|:------:|:---------------:|:-----:|:------:|
| S001 | 1xy | 100-199 | ⬜ Historical |
| S002 | 2xy | 200-299 | ✅ CLOSED |
| S003 | 3xy | 300-399 | ✅ CLOSED |
| **S004** | **4xy** | **400-499** | **🟢 ACTIVE (THIS LEARNING)** |
| S005 | 5xy | 500-599 | ⬜ Future |
| S006 | 6xy | 600-699 | ⬜ Future |

### Structure of `4xy`

| Part | Range | Meaning |
|:----:|:-----:|---------|
| **4** | Fixed | Season 004 identifier |
| **x** | 0-9 | Category sub-range (L, R, BP, D) |
| **y** | 0-9 | Sequence within category |

> **Note:** The `4xy` notation is standardized throughout this document. Earlier drafts used `4xy` and `4xx` interchangeably — **corrected in r4 per Calhoun 🎖️ recommendation.**

## 📋 Grandfather Clause — Pre-4xy Artifacts

### The Rule

> **Legacy S004 artifacts assigned BEFORE the 4xy convention (L-468) are GRANDFATHERED — NOT subject to renumbering.** They retain their original numbers and remain valid under their original naming convention.

### Grandfathered Items

| Category | Grandfathered Numbers | Reason |
|:--------:|:---------------------:|--------|
| **L (Learnings)** | L-225 → L-231 | L-225 (Governance Numbering Authority) + L-226→L-231 — **✅ L-225 added in r4 per Calhoun 🎖️ finding** |
| **BP (Best Practices)** | BP-075 (RAG Fidelity Verification Protocol) | Assigned by Surge ⚡ under 0xy naming — predates 4xy |
| **D (Definitions)** | D-062 → D-064 | S003-era definitions — predate S004 entirely |
| **R-CCC (Custom Prefix)** | R-CCC-1 → R-CCC-4 | Custom prefix, valid per existing governance |

### What This Means

| Action | Grandfathered? |
|--------|:--------------:|
| Keep existing numbers | ✅ YES |
| Reference in new documents | ✅ YES |
| Renumber to 4xy | ❌ NOT REQUIRED |
| Update citations | 🟡 RECOMMENDED but NOT required |
| Extend the grandfathered range | ❌ NO — closed. New items MUST use 4xy |

### Why L-225 Was Added (Calhoun 🎖️ Finding)

During #MetaCouncil VSA, Calhoun 🎖️ identified:

> "The grandfather clause grandfathers L-226→L-231. But L-225 (Governance Numbering Authority, Surge) — is that grandfathered? The clause says L-226→L-231, NOT L-225. So L-225 may fall outside both the grandfather range AND the 4xy range."

**Fix:** Grandfather clause expanded to L-225→L-231 in r4. L-225 (governance numbering authority) is a pre-4xy S004 artifact and correctly protected.
